My name is Krista Chapman

I am KCS Behavior Liaison Specialist and I LOVE my job! I get to work with the most amazing kids in the world on a daily basis! I have been with Kannapolis City Schools for over 7 years and I could not imagine working anywhere else. I was honored in September 2011 with KCS Teacher of the Year recognition.

Kannapolis City Schools

Kannapolis City Schools is a local education agency headquartered in Kannapolis, North Carolina. It encompasses parts of Cabarrus and Rowan Counties, yet operates independently of both county-wide school systems. This is a legacy of the fact that Kannapolis was originally a company town for Cannon Mills.
